OUR MISSION

Breaking the cycle of youth disconnection by employing at-risk youth as technology coaches for an aging population.



WHAT WE DO

Connecting potential...

  • 6.7 million youth between ages 16-24 are neither working nor in school
  • Millennials are so accustomed to the internet that they would rather give up plumbing, heating and air conditioning, personal transportation and cable TV before they would go without connectivity and the electricity needed to power their devices.

...to opportunity:

  • More than 10,000 people turn 65 each day
  • 77% of seniors say they would need someone to help walk them through the process of learning a new technology device
  • 56% of seniors report that they would need assistance if they wanted to use social networking sites to connect with friends or family members
